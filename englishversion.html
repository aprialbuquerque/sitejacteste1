    About the School

    Located in the heart of Copacabana neighborhood, we offer excellent infrastructure for your dance classes. With air-conditioned environment and classes full of excitement, we guarantee a unique experience.

    It's also a place with easy access, close to the subway, bus stops, and in front of an Itaú Bike station. Additionally, we have a comfortable changing room, ideal for those coming straight from work and wishing to change clothes conveniently in a clean environment.

    Here at Jaime Arôxa Copacabana Dance School, you'll feel at home, in a family environment where passion for dance brings people of all ages and backgrounds together.

    Come be part of our family and discover the joy of dancing with us!

    Adress: Avenida Nossa Senhora de Copacabana, 1138 - sobreloja, Copacabana, Rio de Janeiro/RJ.

    BAILE DO TUM E TUM

    The event operates as a monthly gathering hosted by the dance school, aimed at providing students with an opportunity to practice what they have learned throughout the month alongside peers from other classes and even different schools. It fosters a fun, welcoming, and familial atmosphere where everyone dances with each other. This event is designed for both beginners and intermediate dancers, offering a conducive environment for learning and improvement.

    TUM E TUM DAS ESTRELAS

    The "Tum e Tum das Estrelas" it's a party with free drinks and food included in the ticket price. Additionally, there will be performances by the school's students, encouraging them to be more involved in dance by rehearsing choreographies. Furthermore, it follows the same format as the school event, but on a larger scale, at a different time and location. This expansion aims to offer students a fresh experience while maintaining the familiar atmosphere and opportunities for learning and socializing.
